How to Play

This game is completely up to you and the choices you make.

The main objective is to assist Astra and his team ‘The Orbit Rangers’ to help save the planet Pokitaru as it’s currently under attack by the evil empire The Magnetars led by Vex.

Upon your journey you will encounter obstacles that could get in your way, you will need to choose wisely to safely navigate to Pokitaru.

One more thing you should pay attention to is the fuel and an ammo bar, you will need to keep a close eye on these as you don’t want to run out at a crucial part of the game!

Good Luck Ranger!
